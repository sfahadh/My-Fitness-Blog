Heart Rate Variability and It's Role with the Autonomic Nervous System

Heart rate Variability (HRV) is distance between the peak of the QRS wave to the next, also known as the R-R interval. In a normal healthy heart, there should be Variability in the time of the heartbeats because it shows that it is responding to internal and external stressors. HRV can be used to track recovery (great for athletes to prevent overtraining) and how healthy your heart is.
